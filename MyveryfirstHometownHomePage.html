Three Famous Place to Visit

Vishwa Shanti Stupa, Large white Peace Pagoda in Rajgir, Nalanda District, Bihar, near Gitai Mandir. Statues of the Buddha are mounted on the stupa in four directions. It also has a small Japanese Buddhist temple with a large park.

Mahabodhi Temple, Bodhgaya , Situated on the banks of the river the Niranjana River in Bodhgaya, this UNESCO World Heritage Site is a holy place for not just Buddhist for everyone in search of spiritual peace.

Vaidyanatha Jyotirlinga temple , also known as Baba Baidyanath dham and Baidyanath dham is one of the twelve Jyotirlingas, the most sacred abodes of Shiva.
